About Sound Level Measurement

Measure ambient noise in decibels. Prolonged exposure above 85dB causes hearing damage.

Check your hearing range periodically.

Understanding Decibels

Quiet (0-40dB)

Library (30dB), bedroom (35dB). Ideal for recording.

Moderate (40-70dB)

Conversation (60dB), office (50dB). Comfortable for activities.

Loud (70-120dB)

Traffic (80dB), concerts (100dB). Protection needed above 85dB.
