Before You Contact Us

If you're experiencing issues with your microphone or audio devices, please check our Troubleshooting Guide first. It covers solutions for the most common audio problems including:

  • Microphone not detected by browser
  • No audio input or low volume
  • Echo or feedback issues - try our echo test
  • High background noise - use our noise analyzer
  • Audio latency problems - check with latency test
  • Speaker or headphone issues

What to Include in Your Message

To help us assist you better, please include:

  • Browser & Version: e.g., Chrome 120, Firefox 121, Safari 17
  • Operating System: e.g., Windows 11, macOS Sonoma, Android 14
  • Device Type: Desktop, laptop, smartphone, tablet
  • Microphone/Speaker: Built-in, USB, Bluetooth, etc.
  • Which tool you were using: e.g., Mic Test, Recorder, Stereo Test
  • Description of the issue: What happened vs. what you expected
  • Any error messages: If applicable
