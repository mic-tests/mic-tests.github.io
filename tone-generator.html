Frequency Guide
  • 20-60 Hz: Sub-bass, feel more than hear
  • 60-250 Hz: Bass, warmth of sound
  • 250-2000 Hz: Midrange, voice clarity
  • 2000-4000 Hz: Presence, definition
  • 4000-20000 Hz: Brilliance, air
Safety Notice

Start with low volume. High frequencies at high volume can cause hearing damage. If testing bass frequencies, be careful with speaker/subwoofer limits.
