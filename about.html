About MicTest

Your trusted platform for free online audio testing tools.

What is MicTest?

MicTest is a comprehensive online platform that provides free, browser-based audio testing tools. Our microphone test allows you to check if your microphone is working properly without downloading or installing any third-party software.

Whether you're preparing for an important video call, setting up a podcast, or troubleshooting audio issues, MicTest provides the tools you need. Our platform works with any microphone type - built-in laptop mics, USB microphones, wireless Bluetooth headsets, and professional XLR setups.

Our Audio Testing Tools

MicTest offers a complete suite of audio testing and analysis tools:

Audio Input Tools
  • Microphone Test - Test your mic with real-time visualization and recording
  • Show My Mic - View detailed microphone specifications and capabilities
  • Mic Recorder - Record your voice and download as MP3/WAV
  • Sound Level Meter - Measure ambient sound levels in decibels
  • Echo Test - Detect and diagnose echo issues
  • Background Noise Analyzer - Analyze ambient noise in your environment
  • Voice Frequency Analyzer - Real-time voice frequency spectrum analysis
  • Audio Latency Test - Measure microphone input latency
Audio Output Tools
  • Show My Speakers - View speaker and audio output device information
  • Tone Generator - Generate sine waves, white noise, and test tones
  • Stereo Test - Test left and right speaker channels
  • Bass Test - Test subwoofer and low frequency response
  • Speaker Volume Test - Calibrate speaker volume levels
Device Testing
  • Headphone Test - Comprehensive headphone audio testing
  • Hearing Test - Simple online hearing range test

Key Features

  • No Downloads Required - Everything works in your browser
  • Privacy First - Audio is processed locally, never uploaded
  • Cross-Platform - Works on Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, Android
  • All Browsers - Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge compatible
  • Detailed Analysis - View technical specs and quality ratings
  • 100% Free - All tools available at no cost

How It Works

Our tools use the Web Audio API and WebRTC technology built into modern browsers. When you test your microphone, the audio is captured and analyzed entirely within your browser - no data is sent to our servers.

This approach ensures complete privacy while providing accurate, real-time audio analysis. You can measure audio levels, visualize frequencies, detect latency, and diagnose common audio issues all without installing any software.
